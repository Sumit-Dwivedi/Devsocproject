Frequently Asked Questions

What is Align?

Align is an online student collaboration platform designed to help students work together on projects, share study materials, and connect with professionals.

How can I collaborate on projects using Align?

With Align, you can create or join project groups, invite fellow students to collaborate, share files, assign tasks, and communicate effectively to work together towards project completion.

Can I share study materials and resources on Align?

Absolutely! Align provides a platform where you can easily upload and share study materials, notes, resources, and other relevant documents with your peers.

How can I connect with professionals on Align?

Align offers opportunities to connect with industry professionals who can provide guidance, mentorship, and insights. You can explore professional profiles, reach out to them, and seek their assistance for your projects or career development.
